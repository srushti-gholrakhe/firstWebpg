Onion Fritters(pakoras)

It is an Indian dish.It is a snack which is mostly made in monsoon season.It is crispy and can have with Mint-cilantro chutney/dip.

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • chickpea flour
  • chillies
  • salt
  • turmeric
  • coriander
  • Oil
  • Red chili powder
  • baking soda

Instructions

  1. Wash and Chop onion in half circle.
  2. Mix Chopped onions and Chickpea flour.
  3. Add Spices like salt,turmeric,red chili powder,chopped coriander,baking soda.
  4. mixture should have sticky coating on the onion slices.Make sure that,it should be thick.Not runny like pancake batter.
  5. Make small fritters of mixture and Deep fry until it they turn golden brown and crispy.
  6. Serve Fritters while they are Hot.
